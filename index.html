About

"Good design is as little design as possible." — Dieter Rams

I've spent 8+ years shaping products across fintech, B2B SaaS, and consumer experiences—working closely with engineering to design systems that endure. I focus on the decisions people can't always name but feel: a word, an animation, the space between elements. These are the details that make a product feel resolved.

AI expands what's possible for a designer. I use it to explore directions, challenge assumptions, and push ideas further before committing.

Previous work includes Spritz Finance, Journey (YC W21), Peach Payments, Kurtosys, and MakeReign.

When I'm not designing, I spend my days near the ocean, reading, surfing, walking my dog, and thinking about how the world might be better designed.
