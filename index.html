About

Hi, I’m Luke. I design things. Then I build them.

I have 8+ years in product design and a background in software development — and AI has pulled me back to my roots. I now spend less time designing screens and more time building real products, using AI tools to move fast, think clearly about systems, and ship things that create genuine value.

Right now I’m focused on my own products, working zero to one. No clients, no briefs — just early-stage building.

Previously, I worked with Spritz Finance, Journey (YC), Peach Payments, Kurtosys, and MakeReign — across product design, strategy, and financial products.

Outside of work, I split my time between Cape Town and the road, with books, long walks with my dog, and the ocean.
