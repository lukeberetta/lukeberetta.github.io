About

Hi, I'm Luke. I design things, then I build them.

I spent 8+ years as a product designer, working closely with engineers to ship digital products that solve real problems. Over time I developed a strong foundation in software development, which means I think about products as systems — not just screens.

AI has pulled me back toward building.

These days I focus on creating my own products, going from zero to one using modern AI tools. Less time in design files, more time shipping working software.

Previously I worked with companies like Spritz Finance, Journey (YC), Peach Payments, Kurtosys, and MakeReign — across product design, strategy, and financial technology.

Outside of work I split my time between Cape Town and the road, usually with a book, my dog, and the ocean nearby.
